\section{Log Likelihood}

Log likelihood $\ln(L)$ calculation, where $\Delta x$ is the error in the data
$x$ and $m(t)$ is the model at time $t$:
\begin{equation}
    \ln(L) = -0.5 \sum {({x(t) - m(t))^2} \over {\Delta x^2}}
\end{equation}
